Best Family Holiday Activities in Crete

Your essential guide to fun-filled family experiences on Greece’s largest island

1. Explore the Palace of Knossos

Step into ancient history at the Palace of Knossos, the centre of the Minoan civilisation. Kids will love the myths of King Minos and the Minotaur as they explore the palace’s colourful frescoes and maze-like corridors.

2. Enjoy a Day at Crete’s Family-Friendly Beaches

Crete boasts some of the safest, cleanest beaches in Greece. Elafonissi Beach, with its pink sands and shallow waters, is ideal for young children. Falassarna and Georgioupolis also offer gentle waves and plenty of beachside tavernas.

3. Take a Boat Trip to Spinalonga Island

Families can board a boat from Elounda or Agios Nikolaos to visit Spinalonga Island. This historic island fortress offers captivating stories and breathtaking views — plus the boat ride itself is a highlight for little ones.

4. Discover Crete’s Caves

The Dikteon Cave, said to be the birthplace of Zeus, is a thrilling spot to spark kids' imaginations. Nearby Sfendoni Cave also offers easy access and guided tours suitable for families.

5. Visit the CretAquarium

Perfect for a cloudy day, CretAquarium near Heraklion introduces children to the wonders of the Mediterranean Sea. With hundreds of marine species on display, it’s both educational and fun.

6. Go Hiking in Imbros Gorge

For families with older children, the Imbros Gorge offers an easier alternative to the famous Samaria Gorge. The 8 km walk through dramatic scenery is manageable and incredibly rewarding.
